MY LEARNING PLAN

My Bootcamp learning plan


In my long-term career pathway, I am aiming to pursue a front-end web development role at an entry level. I believe that this field aligns well with my interests and strengths, and I am excited to learn and grow in this dynamic industry.

During the Bootcamp, I anticipate that my biggest strengths will lie in active listening and being able to adapt and learn quickly. However, I am aware that there are a few limitations I need to overcome. One of them is the tendency to get stuck on a particular section for an extended period while trying to figure it out. I understand the importance of time management and problem-solving efficiency, and I am committed to improving in these areas.

Apart from technical challenges, I anticipate that my biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be speaking in front of others or presenting my work. As someone who may be naturally more introverted, I recognize the value of effective communication and the ability to confidently articulate ideas. I am eager to develop these skills and become more comfortable with public speaking throughout the program.

In terms of non-technical skills, or human skills, I would like to focus on improving my communication abilities, both in terms of expressing myself clearly and actively listening to others. Additionally, I would like to enhance my skills in pair programming, as effective collaboration and providing constructive feedback are vital in a team-oriented environment. I am determined to work on these areas of limitation and actively contribute to the learning and growth of my classmates as well.

As for my expectations from the Bootcamp team, I anticipate that they will be available and supportive whenever I require assistance. I hope that the team fosters a collaborative and inclusive learning environment, where we can help one another progress and succeed. I believe that a strong support system is crucial for the success of each participant, and I look forward to engaging with the Bootcamp team in a positive and constructive manner.

Regarding my expectations of myself during Bootcamp, I have set high standards. I am committed to staying on track with the program's curriculum and dedicating myself to my studies for the entire duration of the nine weeks. I understand the importance of focus and discipline, and I am determined to resist distractions and maintain a strong work ethic. I believe that by holding myself accountable and consistently putting in the effort, I can make the most of the Bootcamp experience and achieve my goals.





A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning.


When it comes to learning, one of my key strengths would be my eagerness and curiosity to explore new areas of knowledge. I feel comfortable in situations where I can explore new topics and expand my knowledge in different areas. However, one of my limitations is the amount of time it takes for me to become fully focused on studying. I sometimes need a transition period to develop a focused mindset. Once I get past this initial phase, I am able to immerse myself in the subject matter and give my full attention to the learning process. Recognizing this limitation, I strive to implement strategies and techniques to help me improve my concentration and minimize distractions, so that I can achieve a state of increased focus and productivity when studying.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?


During my time at Dev Academy, I would like to develop several non-technical core skills to increase my overall effectiveness. First and foremost, I want to overcome my fear of meeting new people, especially as an introvert. By challenging myself to step out of my comfort zone, engage in networking opportunities, and actively participate in social situations, I want to build confidence and make meaningful connections with other students, mentors, and industry professionals. I also understand the importance of effective collaboration and teamwork in a professional setting. To further develop my ability to work effectively with team members on group projects, I want to enhance my communication skills, active listening, and empathy. By actively participating in group discussions, taking different perspectives, and constructively contributing constructively to the team's common goals, I want to foster a collaborative environment where ideas can flourish, and shared successes can be achieved.





A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.


In order to effectively manage my workload in this program, I have made a commitment to fully engage by resigning from my full-time job. This allows me to prioritize my learning and actively engage with other peers and facilitators. Whenever I feel unsure or overwhelmed, I will seek support from my peers and facilitators, leveraging their advice and expertise. I also understand the importance of taking breaks and stepping away from the screen when things start to pile up and stress takes its toll. By allowing myself time to rest and recharge, I can regain clarity and get back to my work with renewed focus and energy.





A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way.


I commit to seeking help promptly when needed, knowing that timely assistance is critical to my success. I will actively engage with my peers and moderators on Discord and Slack, seeking their advice and expertise to overcome challenges and clarify any uncertainties I may have. In addition to the community, I will also utilize the many resources on platforms such as YouTube and Google to find solutions.





A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team.


Primarily, I anticipate receiving valuable advice and guidance from the facilitators, drawing from their expertise and industry knowledge. I also look forward to collaborating with my peers, as I believe in the power of collective learning. Moreover, I hope to learn from their experiences, benefiting from their insights and gaining a broader perspective on the subject matter. Ultimately, I trust the Facilitation team's ability to create an engaging and supportive environment that fosters growth and skill development.





Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.


In order to maintain a balanced schedule, I have certain off-screen commitments that I prioritize. For example, I allocate dedicated time to going to the gym because that is part of my physical well-being. I believe it's important to take care of my health to increase my overall productivity and focus. I also consciously avoid distractions during my allotted study times to ensure that I can fully focus on the learning process. By scheduling and keeping these commitments, I aim to create a structured and focused environment that optimizes my learning experience.
